Offradio is the leading professional online radio in Greece, with a full live daily schedule, popular producers and international eclectic music content.

The app was designed to serve Offradio's listeners with the ability to listen to their favorite streaming radio station on their iPhone, alongside the station's web player on www.offradio.gr/player.

Features:
- New updated design
- See what’s playing and share it on Twitter and Facebook, or via e-mail
- View what played, by accessing Offradio playlist
- Automatically search for the playlist’s tracks on iTunes
- View the daily shows schedule
- View the producers profiles
- Contact the producers via Offradio social media channels
